PIAIC

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing

The Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing (PIAIC) is a privately funded, not-for-profit educational initiative started by the President of Pakistan, Dr. Arif Alvi, Kazi Rahat Ali, and Zia Khan. The aim is to promote education, research, and business opportunities in emerging tech fields like:

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain
  • Internet of Things

Cloud Native Computing:

The Cloud Native Computing program prepares students to program in open-source technologies like Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript, Node.js, React, and React Native. It introduces students to developing microservices and software development techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence:

The Artificial Intelligence program takes students through the foundations of AI technology. It teaches programming in open-source languages like Python, Numpy, Pandas, TensorFlow 2.0, and Flask, and focuses on deploying practical AI solutions with real-world examples of AI in practice.

Blockchain:

The Blockchain program emphasizes understanding blockchains like Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric) and programming in languages such as Solidity and JavaScript. It covers frameworks like Node.js, DApps development, and Blockchain-as-a-Service (BaaS), as well as Initial Coin Offerings (ICO) for crypto-tokens.

Internet of Things (IoT):

The Internet of Things program introduces students to open-source technologies like Rust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, Hyper, Diesel, Google Assistant, and Kubernetes. It focuses on developing microservices for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud, and voice computing for device control.
